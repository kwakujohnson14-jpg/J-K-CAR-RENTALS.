     Background Information
    

    J&K CAR RENTALS was founded in 2024 with the vision of transforming
    mobility solutions in Ghana. Recognizing the growing need for affordable,reliable,
    and flexible transportation, the company was established to bridge
    the gap between convenience and quality service in the car rental industry.
    With a modern fleet of vehicles and a customer-first approach,J&K provides
    a seamless experience for both short-term and long-term rentals
